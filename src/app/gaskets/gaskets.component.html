Butyl & Neoprene Gaskets


Uni-Butyl™ XT Butyl Gasket is a 100 percent cross-linked butyl gasket tape, designed for use on four-bolt flange connector systems. It is non-hardening, has excellent adhesion to metal surfaces, and has outstanding resistance to extreme temperatures.
