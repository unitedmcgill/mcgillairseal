Silicone Sealant


Uni-Sil™ Silicone Sealer is a one-part sealant that cures in the presence of atmospheric moisture to produce a durable and flexible silicone rubber. Because it has excellent adhesion, it can be used in a variety of environments including general-purpose construction and industrial applications. Uni-Sil can be used for sealing clean metal, glass, wood, and plastic surfaces.

Available in 10.3-ounce tubes, 1-gallon plastic pails, and 52-gallon drums.
