Polyurethane Sealant


Uni-Thane™ Polyurethane Sealant is a one-part, non-sag, permanently flexible, multi-purpose sealant and adhesive. It is paintable and has excellent adhesion to metals, wood, glass, and plastics. It is ideal for sealing indoor/outdoor joints with dynamic and thermal movement.

Available in 1- and 5-gallon metal pails.
