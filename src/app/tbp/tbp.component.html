Tape, Butyl, Polyurethane Sealant


Uni-Cast® Tape Sealing System is an economical method for sealing HVAC duct systems. It combines a woven-fiber tape with an acrylic liquid adhesive to form a hard, durable, airtight seal. The unique Uni-Cast applicator is easy to use and simplifies cleanup. Uni-Cast is nontoxic, nonflammable, and contains no asbestos. It is suitable for indoor and outdoor applications to seal low-, medium-, and high-pressure air distribution systems. Uni-Cast can be used on any shape of duct (round, flat oval, or rectangular). Once applied, it is not damaged by sub-zero temperatures or ultraviolet light.

The adhesive is available in 1-gallon containers, and the tape is available in boxes of six rolls. Each roll is 3 inches wide by 150 feet in length.

Uni-Seam™ Cold Seam Sealer is a nonhardening solvent-based polymeric rubber mastic. It is ideal for sealing longitudinal seams on rectangular duct (e.g. Pittsburgh style).

Available in 10.3-ounce tubes, 5-gallon pails, and 52-gallon drums.

Uni-Thane™ Polyurethane Sealant is a one-part, non-sag, permanently flexible, multi-purpose sealant and adhesive. It is paintable and has excellent adhesion to metals, wood, glass, and plastics. It is ideal for sealing indoor/outdoor joints with dynamic and thermal movement.

Available in 10.3-oz tubes, 20-oz packs, 1- and 5-gallon metal pails, and drums.
