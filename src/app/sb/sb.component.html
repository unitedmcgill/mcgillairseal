Solvent-Based Sealant


United Duct Sealer™ has a high bonding strength for sure, first-time sealing of joints in low-, medium-, and high-pressure HVAC metal duct systems. It is suitable for indoor and outdoor applications. It has a high solids content and is less subject to rapid evaporation than standard sealants. Its ready-to-use consistency will not drip or run, making application easier and faster.

Available in 10.5- and 29-ounce cartridges, 1-gallon cans, 5-gallon pails, and 52-gallon drums.

Uni-Seal™ Duct Sealer is an economical high-strength sealant for use on sheet metal duct systems. This medium-viscosity sealant is easy to apply. It dries quickly and remains flexible to provide a sure seal.

Available in 10.5- and 29-ounce cartridges, 1-gallon cans, 5-gallon pails, and 52-gallon drums.

Uni-Seal™ Duct Sealer Safety information

Uni-Weather™ Duct Sealer is a medium-viscosity sealant for indoor/outdoor application on duct systems. It is specially formulated to resist weathering and aging, even when exposed to ultraviolet light.

Available in 10.5-ounce cartridges, 1-gallon cans, 5-gallon pails, and 52-gallon drums.

Uni-Weather™ Duct Sealer Safety information

Uni-Coat™ Duct Sealer (aka Foremost Duct Sealer or PCD Sealer #8) is an all-weather indoor/outdoor sealant that is specially formulated to resist weathering and aging, even when exposed to ultraviolet light and underground conditions as on PVC plastic-coated steel. It is good for low- to high-pressure duct systems. Uni-Coat is UL classified.

Available in 10.5-oz tubes and 1- and 5-gallon metal pails and drums.
