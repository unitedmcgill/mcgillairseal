Aerosol Insulation Adhesives


Uni-Spray™ Insulation Adhesive provides a high-strength bond between metal and fiberglass. It provides instant tack, yet is repositional, making it ideal for shop or field application. Bonds to wood, concrete, carpet, paper, fabric, and most plastics.

Available in 20-ounce aerosol cans.

Uni-Spray™ Insulation Adhesive Safety information

Uni-Spray™ C Insulation Adhesive is an industrial-strength adhesive that creates a permanent or temporary bond. It is portable, self-contained spray system that is an alternative to hand or pump application. Bonds to insulation, metals, fabric, foam, glass, and plastics.

Available in 11-, 40-, 187-, and 375-lb cylinders.

Uni-Spray™ C Adhesive Safety information

Uni-Spray™ NF Insulation Adhesive is a non-flammable adhesive that creates a permanent or temporary bond. It is a portable, self-contained spray system that is an alternative to hand or pump application. Bonds to insulation, metals, fabric, foam, glass, and plastics.

Available in 11-, 40-, 187-, and 375-lb cylinders.
