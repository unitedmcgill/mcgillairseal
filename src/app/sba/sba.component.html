Solvent-Based Insulation Adhesives


Uni-Fast™ Duct Liner Adhesive is an economical, solvent-based product that provides a high-strength bond between metal and fiberglass duct liner. Uni-Fast is sprayable and instantly tacky, making it ideal for shop and field application. Uni-Fast can be used for a variety of special applications including bonding to wood, concrete block, carpet, paper, cardboard, fabric, and some plastics.

Available in 1-gallon cans, 5-gallon pails, and 52-gallon drums.
